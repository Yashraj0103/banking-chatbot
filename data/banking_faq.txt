PERSONAL LOANS
==============
Q: What is a personal loan?
A: A personal loan is an unsecured loan provided by banks to individuals for personal use such as medical expenses, travel, or home renovation. No collateral is required.

Q: What is the interest rate for personal loans?
A: Personal loan interest rates typically range from 10.5% to 24% per annum depending on your credit score, income, and the bank's policy.

Q: What is the maximum personal loan amount?
A: Most banks offer personal loans ranging from Rs.50,000 to Rs.40,00,000 based on your monthly income and repayment capacity.

Q: What documents are required for a personal loan?
A: Required documents: Aadhaar card, PAN card, last 3 months salary slips, last 6 months bank statements, and passport-size photographs.

Q: What is the repayment tenure for a personal loan?
A: Personal loans can be repaid over 12 to 60 months (1 to 5 years).

CREDIT CARDS
============
Q: How do I apply for a credit card?
A: Apply online via the bank website or visit a branch. You need stable income, credit score above 700, and valid KYC documents.

Q: What is a credit limit?
A: Credit limit is the maximum amount you can spend on your credit card, set by the bank based on your income and credit history.

Q: What happens if I miss a credit card payment?
A: Missing a payment incurs a late fee and interest charges of 2 to 3.5% per month. It also negatively impacts your CIBIL score.

Q: What is the grace period for credit card payments?
A: Most banks offer a grace period of 18 to 25 days after the billing cycle ends to pay without interest.

SAVINGS ACCOUNT
===============
Q: What is the minimum balance for a savings account?
A: Minimum balance varies by bank: typically Rs.1,000 to Rs.10,000 for regular accounts. Zero-balance accounts are also available.

Q: What is the interest rate on a savings account?
A: Savings account interest rates range from 2.5% to 7% per annum. Interest is calculated daily and credited quarterly.

Q: How do I open a savings account?
A: Visit any branch with Aadhaar card, PAN card, passport photo, and an initial deposit. Online account opening is also available via the bank app.

FIXED DEPOSITS
==============
Q: What is a Fixed Deposit?
A: A Fixed Deposit (FD) is a scheme where you deposit a lump sum for a fixed period at a predetermined interest rate. It is safe and guaranteed.

Q: What are FD interest rates?
A: FD rates range from 5.5% to 8.5% per annum. Senior citizens get an additional 0.25 to 0.50%.

Q: Can I break my FD before maturity?
A: Yes, with a penalty of 0.5% to 1% on the applicable interest rate.

Q: What is the minimum FD amount?
A: Most banks allow FDs starting from Rs.1,000 with tenures from 7 days to 10 years.
