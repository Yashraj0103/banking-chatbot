HOME LOAN POLICY
================
Eligibility:
- Minimum age 21 years, maximum age 65 years at loan maturity
- Minimum monthly income Rs.25,000 for salaried individuals
- Minimum 2 years of continuous work experience

Loan Details:
- Maximum amount: up to 80% of property value
- Tenure: 5 to 30 years
- Interest rate: 8.5% to 10.5% per annum (floating or fixed)
- Processing fee: 0.5% of loan amount

Required Documents:
- Salary slips for last 3 months
- ITR for last 2 years (self-employed)
- Property documents
- Aadhaar and PAN card
- Bank statements for 12 months

EDUCATION LOAN POLICY
=====================
Eligibility:
- Indian nationals with confirmed admission to a recognised institution
- Age between 16 and 35 years

Loan Details:
- Up to Rs.10 lakh for studies in India
- Up to Rs.30 lakh for studies abroad
- Repayment starts 1 year after course completion or 6 months after getting a job

Interest Rates:
- 9% to 13.5% depending on loan amount and institution

CAR LOAN POLICY
===============
Eligibility:
- Minimum age 21 years
- Minimum income Rs.20,000 per month
- Good credit score (above 650)

Loan Details:
- Finance up to 90% of vehicle on-road price
- Tenure: 1 to 7 years
- Interest rate: 7.5% to 12% per annum
- Loan amount: Rs.1 lakh to Rs.50 lakh

Required Documents:
- Driving licence
- Income proof
- Address proof
- Vehicle quotation from dealer
